See how the Restraint Log App works

A worked example so you can see what the app produces — before you use it for real.

Demo scenario

A young person becomes distressed following a boundary around leaving the home late at night. Staff use verbal reassurance, offer space, reduce the audience, offer choices and attempt distraction. The young person attempts to strike another young person and moves towards the front door. Staff complete a dynamic risk assessment and use a trained low-level touch support and escort to move the young person to a safer area. The intervention lasts 45 seconds and is released as soon as the risk reduces. The young person is offered emotional support afterwards, and the manager reviews the incident.

Guidance & privacy

What this tool is for

This tool supports professional recording. It does not replace organisational policy, safeguarding procedures, Regulation 40 notifications, CQC notifications, incident reporting systems, local authority reporting requirements, medical advice or professional judgement.

Physical intervention should only be used when necessary, proportionate, reasonable and the least restrictive option available in response to immediate risk. Staff should only use techniques they have been trained and assessed as competent to use.

Data & privacy

  • Do not enter unnecessary personal information.
  • Use initials where appropriate.
  • Follow your organisation’s data-protection policy.
  • Only authorised staff should access restraint records.
  • Records must be stored securely.
  • Exported PDFs must be handled confidentially.

Entries you save are stored only in this browser on this device. Nothing is sent to a server.
