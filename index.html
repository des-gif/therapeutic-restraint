The approach

A therapeutic model, not traditional restraint training.

Every element is designed to reduce restrictive intervention — and the harm it can cause — by getting ahead of crisis.

🤝

Therapeutic & person-centred

Protecting dignity, relationships and emotional safety — for the people you support and your staff.

🌱

Positive Behaviour Support

Proactive, PBS-led strategies that reduce challenging behaviour and the need for any restrictive intervention.

🗣️

De-escalation first

Reading triggers and early warning signs, and resolving situations long before restraint is ever considered.

⚖️

Lawful & accountable

Reasonable force, human rights, duty of care and professional accountability — confidently understood.

❤️

Risk-aware practice

The real risks of physical intervention, including positional asphyxia, and how to avoid causing harm.

🔄

Reflection & learning

Trauma-informed, post-incident reflection and best practice that keeps your service improving.
